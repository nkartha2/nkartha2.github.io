Goal

The goal of this project was to create a responsive fundraising web page for World Bicycle Relief’s first program in Kenya. World Bicycle Relief is an organization that works globally to provide bicycles to students, healthcare workers, and entrepreneurs.The Kenyan program is solely focused on providing bicycles to students.

  • Applying and customizing jQuery plugins
  • Building a gif in Adobe Photoshop and Adobe After Effects

Recap

We began this project by creating an interview guide and then interviewing potential donors. We wanted to know people's motivations when donating to a charity and what donors look for in an a charity organization's website when making the decision to donate. We also performed a heuristic evaluation of other charitable organizations’ websites. After the research sprint finished, we created an affinity map to draw our final design principles.

After the research and design phases were completed and deliverables were approved by the client, I then developed the final responsive site. I also created the animated gif of moving bicycle wheels in order to invite users to interact with the sliding story.
